📏 Selection Rules (2-3-2)
==========================================

Required:
├── 2 golfers from Top 10 (Rank 1-10)
├── 3 golfers from Mid 40 (Rank 11-50)
└── 2 golfers from Field (Rank 51+)

Scoring:
├── Your score = Sum of BEST 4 golfers
├── Lower is better (golf scoring)
├── Missed cut golfers count at current score
└── Leaderboard updates live
